Let’s Get to Know About
Ambulance Services

Our organization’s mission is to provide dedicated and outstanding medical transportation services, and solutions for Medical Facilities and their Patients by utilizing our industry experience and expertise in medical transportation and home health services to assist them with the efficient movement of patients and ultimately meeting the strategic goals of our clients expediently and cost-efficiently. APEX Medical Transportation understands that when our patients are at their most vulnerable, our level of care and service should be exemplary. We recognize the power of people, from our cadre of employees and EMT’s, to the clients we serve. APEX supports several area Medical Centres with a commitment to high standards and integrity in delivering services.
