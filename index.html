About Our Ambulance Services

Advance Life Support

Our Advanced Life Support (ALS) Ambulance Service is designed to provide critical care and rapid response for patients with serious medical conditions. Staffed by highly trained paramedics and equipped with cutting-edge medical technology, our ALS ambulances ensure that patients receive advanced medical interventions during transportation.

Basic Life Support

At Apex Medical Transport, our Basic Life Support (BLS) Ambulance Service offers essential medical care and transportation for patients who require monitoring and support but are not in immediate life-threatening situations. Staffed by skilled EMTs, our BLS ambulances are equipped with the necessary tools to provide vital services

Paramedics & EMT

Apex's EMT & Paramedics Ambulance Service provides an elevated level of pre-hospital care for patients in critical and emergency situations, with highly skilled paramedics who are trained to perform advanced medical procedures, including intravenous therapy, cardiac monitoring, defibrillation, and advanced airway management.
